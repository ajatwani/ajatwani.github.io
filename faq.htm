FAQ's

What is Capsule?

Capsule App is a simplified free accounting, HR, Discount based e-commerce and rating mobile application for small and medium businesses

What is the significance behind the name Capsule?

It's called Capsule because it's like a medicine to the lack of financial records by small and medium businesses and their resultant exclusion from financial services which they actually require to grow, expand and contribute in Job creation thereby contributing in lifting people out of poverty across Africa

Why is Capsule Free?

Capsule App is free and will forever remain free because it was created by a nonprofit organization called Center for Strategic Enterprise Development Ltd/GTE

What are the career opportunities within Capsule?

You could join our fast growing team as an employee or a certified Consultant/Partner

What is Capsule Certified Consultants and Capsule Certified Partners?

CCCs are individuals while CCPs are institutions with CCCs that train business owners on how to use Capsule App as well as provide other consultancy services related to Capsule App to small businesses either at free or fee depending on the Consultants or partners' model

How can I become a Capsule Certified Consultant (CCC) or Capsule Certified Partner (CCP)

Click here to apply for CCC or CCP training and Certification Examination here

What is Capsule's revenue model?

Capsule App revenue model is integrated within the optional advanced features of the App which does not affect the free features of the app listed above; the App is not primarily a money making venture because of the involvement of a non-profit organization in the management however, the advanced features are integrated to sustain the App infrastructures in order to have the App offering services for free.
